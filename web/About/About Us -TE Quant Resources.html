About TE Quant

Get to Know Us
We started as an educational center in the city of Port Harcourt,
aiming to provide quality training to those in the garden city of Nigeria.
It's been over four years and we are still in the business
of providing quality education for professionals.
We began with just one course, but we currently have
multiple courses on our Platforms, with technically sound individuals signed on our platforms as tutors.

Our Mission

We are on a mission to make Tech education more effective by ensuring a direct connection between tutor and students. We have a vision to educate the continent of Africa into innovation.
We want to see that everyone gets a good grasp
of what they are taught and it becomes
inevitable that they apply this knowledge to bring solutions to challenges
facing the people of the continent
